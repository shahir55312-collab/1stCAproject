Classic Pancakes

Prep: 10m • Cook: 15m • Serves: 4
Ingredients: eggs, flour, milk, sugar, butter
Steps
  1. Mix dry ingredients, then add milk and eggs. Stir until smooth.
  2. Heat a pan, add butter, pour batter and cook until bubbles form. Flip and cook other side.
  3. Serve warm with syrup or fruit.

Simple Chicken Fried Rice

Prep: 10m • Cook: 15m • Serves: 2-3
Ingredients: cooked rice, chicken, soy sauce, ginger, garlic, vegetables
Steps
  1. Stir-fry diced chicken until cooked. Remove and set aside.
  2. Sauté garlic, ginger and vegetables. Add rice and chicken back.
  3. Season with soy sauce, stir well and serve.

Quick Tomato Basil Pasta

Prep: 5m • Cook: 15m • Serves: 2
Ingredients: pasta, canned tomatoes, garlic, basil, olive oil, cheese
Steps
  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Sauté garlic, add tomatoes and simmer. Stir in basil and olive oil.
  3. Toss pasta with sauce, top with cheese and serve.

Banana Oatmeal

Prep: 2m • Cook: 5m • Serves: 1
Ingredients: oats, banana, milk (or water), honey, cinnamon
Steps
  1. Combine oats and milk in a pot. Cook until thick.
  2. Stir in sliced banana, honey and a pinch of cinnamon.

Roasted Potatoes

Prep: 10m • Cook: 30m • Serves: 4
Ingredients: potatoes, olive oil, salt, pepper, herbs
Steps
  1. Cut potatoes into cubes, toss with oil, salt, pepper and herbs.
  2. Roast in oven at 200°C (400°F) for ~30 minutes until golden.

Simple Greek Salad

Prep: 10m • Cook: 0m • Serves: 2-3
Ingredients: tomato, cucumber, feta, olives, olive oil, lemon
Steps
  1. Chop vegetables, crumble feta, add olives.
  2. Dress with olive oil, lemon juice, salt and pepper. Toss and serve.

Easy Chocolate Mug Cake

Prep: 2m • Cook: 1-2m • Serves: 1
Ingredients: flour, cocoa powder, sugar, milk, egg, butter
Steps
  1. Mix ingredients in a mug and microwave for 1–2 minutes until cooked through.
  2. Let cool slightly and enjoy.
